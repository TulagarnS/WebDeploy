Iced Dalgona Latte


Dalgona Coffee is a cold latte drink that comes with a velvety smooth and sweet coffee foam on top. The combination of cold milk and the bittersweet coffee is simply a match made in heaven.

prep time
5 mins.
cook time
10 mins
serving
1-2 servings

instructions

step 1

Add the sugar, instant coffee, and cold water to a mixing bowl.

step 2

Whisk or use an electric beater to whip the coffee mixture until light and fluffy.

step 3

Add the cinnamon and ginger and continue to whisk until incorporated.

step 4

Fill two glasses with ice and divide the milk and vanilla extract between the two glasses.

step 5

Top each glass with as much whipped gingerbread coffee as desired.

ingredients

1/4 cup granulated sugar

1/4 cup instant espresso (or instant coffee) powder

1/4 cup cold water

1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

1/2 teaspoon ground ginger

1 teaspoon vanilla extract

2 cups milk of choice

Ice

tools

Hand Blender

Large Heavy Pot With Lid

Measuring Spoons

Measuring Cups
