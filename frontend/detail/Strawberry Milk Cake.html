Strawberry Milk Cake


Tres Leches Cake with Strawberries is wonderfully soaked with a mixture of sweetened condensed milk, evaporated milk, and heavy cream. On top a layer of freshhly whipped cream and sliced strawberries. Such a great Spring dessert!

prep time
12 min.
cook time
5 hrs
serving
8 to 10 servings

instructions

step 1

Bake your boxed strawberry cake mix as directed in a 9x13 baking dish. Cool the cake for about an hour.

step 2

In a blender or food processor blend strawberries, condensed milk, evaporated milk, and whole milk until smooth.

step 3

Keep the finished strawberry leche milk mixture refrigerator until ready to use.

step 4

With a fork poke holes throughout the whole cooled cake.

step 5

Pour half strawberry leche "milk" mixture and wait till it absorbs about 5-8 minutes, then top the cake with other half.

step 6

Seal well and cover with plastic wrap. place in the refrigerator for a minimum of 4 hours or overnight.

step 7

Remove from the refrigerator when ready to serve. Top with homemade whipped cream. Top with strawberry crunch crumbles. Garnish with fresh strawberries, if desired.

ingredients

6 eggs (separate whites and snow)

2 cups of tea

2 cups of special wheat flour

1 cup (tea) of boiling milk

1 tablespoon of baking powder

1 tablespoon of butter

2 boxes of clean and chopped strawberries (reserve some to garnish)

1/2 cup of tea

Nest Filling and Covering Ingredients

1 can of condensed milk

tools

Mixing Bowls

9x13 Baking Dish

stand mixer bowl or large mixing bowl (Bowl #2)

large pot that fits Bowl #2 for the bain-marie (Pot B)

Measuring Tools and Fork

cake pan (8 inches/20 cm)

Plastic Wrap
